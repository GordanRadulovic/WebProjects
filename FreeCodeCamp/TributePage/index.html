Veljko Rađenović

The man who fred Orahovac

General Veljko as commander of Special Police Unit

    Here is a timeline of General Veljko's life

  • 1955 - Born in village Brezanik, near town Pec in Sebia
  • 1974 - Finishes Military Accademy in town of Sremska Kamenica, Serbia
  • 1980 - Veljko gets promoted to a head of police station in village Sredska near town of Prizren, Serbia
  • 1992 - Due to his reputation and extraordinary skill he gets promoted to Commander of a Special Anti-Terroristic police unit
  • 1995 - During Bosnian war he got assigned to guard Serbia-Croatia border
  • 1996 - He was assigned to do riot control in Belgrade
  • 1998 - During the Kosovo war, he was leading the Special Police unit which had task of destroying Albanian terrorists who occupied Serbian villages near town of Orahovac. In the same year he freed the town, saving couple hunderds Serb civilians in the proccess.
  • 2005 - His police/military career ends
  • 2012 - Veljko dies of diabetes, after he had his leg amputated
  • 2014 - He was posthumously awarded the Order of Merit
  • 2015 - He was posthumously awarded the Order of Merit in the Field of Defence and Security
